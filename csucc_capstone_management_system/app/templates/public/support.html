Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my project proposal?

Go to your student dashboard, navigate to the “Project Proposal Submission” section, and upload your file.

Can I edit my proposal after submission?

Yes, as long as your adviser has not yet approved it. You can re-upload a new version.

How can I contact my adviser?

Use the “Messages” section in your dashboard to send a message directly to your adviser.

What if I forgot my password?

Go to the login page and click “Forgot Password” to reset your account credentials.
